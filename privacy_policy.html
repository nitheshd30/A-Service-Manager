Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This application is designed to function as a service management tool for your business while maintaining the highest standards of data security.

1. Data Collection & Security

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data from your device. All information you enter (Repairs, Inventory, etc.) is stored securely in your private database (Supabase) and is only accessible by you and your authorized users.

2. Data Retention & Deletion

If you choose to delete your account, your data (Repairs, Inventory, etc.) will be retained for 30 days before being permanently deleted from our servers. This period allows you to contact support if you wish to recover your data. After 30 days, all associated database tables are automatically and permanently removed.

3. Permissions Required

  • Internet: Required to sync with your private database and cloud backup services.
  • Biometric: Used solely for secure and local app login on your device.
  • Storage: Required for generating and saving Excel reports to your device's Downloads folder.

4. Third-Party Services

The application offers optional integration with Google Drive for data backups. These backups are stored in a hidden 'App Data' folder that only this app can access. Your personal files on Google Drive are never accessed, modified, or shared.

5. Policy Consent

By using this app, you agree that your data is managed entirely within the parameters of your own private database infrastructure. We do not use trackers, analytics, or third-party advertising that collects personal information.
